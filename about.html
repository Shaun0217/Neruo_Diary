About The Neuro Diary

A blend of narrative medicine and neuroscience, capturing the heart of patient encounters and the science that shapes them.

Our Story

The Neuro Diary began as a small notebook filled with quick notes, quiet observations, and moments that stood out during days in the clinic and on hospital rounds.

Over time, those notes became stories: about patients whose strength reshaped how I see medicine, about late nights spent tracing the brain’s mysteries, and about the small, human moments that stay with you long after the day ends.

This blog is an open invitation to step into that world, to see what a neurologist sees, and to discover the hidden connections between science, memory, and the human spirit.

Our Mission

To make neurology accessible and human. We believe that medical knowledge should be approachable without losing its depth, and that stories are the bridge between data and empathy.
